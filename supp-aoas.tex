\begin{abstract}
This supplement contains five technical appendices with detailed material on
the following topics:
\begin{itemize}\setlength{\itemindent}{5em}
  \item[A.] Auger observatory exposure;
  \item[B.] Algorithm for Markov chain Monte Carlo;
  \item[C.] Cen~A single-source model;
  \item[D.] Comparison with prior Bayesian work;
  \item[E.] Model checking.
\end{itemize}
Equation and section numbers in this supplement refer to the main paper
except when prefixed by ``S.''
\end{abstract}
